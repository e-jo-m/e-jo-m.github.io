Man, how messed up would it be if objective morality were only partially real? Like, imagine that it is an objective aspect of the fabric of the universe that human suffering is bad, but otherwise classical utilitarianism versus preference utilitarianism are equally valid.

(How do we know this isn't the case? It would explain a lot.)
